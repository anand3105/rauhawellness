========================================
VERCEL ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES
========================================

Copy these variables to your Vercel project:
Dashboard > Settings > Environment Variables

========================================
FOR CUSTOM EMAIL (info@rauhawellness.com)
========================================

Variable: ADMIN_EMAIL
Value: info@rauhawellness.com

Variable: EMAIL_HOST
Value: smtp.hostinger.com
(or your actual SMTP host - check with your email provider)

Variable: EMAIL_PORT
Value: 465
(use 465 for SSL or 587 for TLS)

Variable: EMAIL_SECURE
Value: true
(true for port 465, false for port 587)

Variable: EMAIL_USER
Value: info@rauhawellness.com

Variable: EMAIL_PASS
Value: [YOUR EMAIL PASSWORD]
(your password for info@rauhawellness.com)

========================================
IMPORTANT NOTES
========================================

1. DO NOT set EMAIL_SERVICE when using custom SMTP
2. Get your SMTP host from your email hosting provider:
   - Hostinger: smtp.hostinger.com
   - cPanel: mail.yourdomain.com
   - Check your hosting control panel
3. Set these for ALL environments (Production, Preview, Development)
4. After adding variables, redeploy your application

========================================
HOW TO FIND YOUR SMTP SETTINGS
========================================

Login to your email hosting provider and look for:
- "Email Settings"
- "SMTP Configuration"
- "Outgoing Mail Server"
- Or contact support: "What are my SMTP settings?"

Common SMTP hosts:
- Hostinger: smtp.hostinger.com
- cPanel: mail.rauhawellness.com
- GoDaddy: smtpout.secureserver.net
- Namecheap: mail.privateemail.com
